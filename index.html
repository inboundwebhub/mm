What is their story?

The Munchie Mafia overall will become a foundational community within the blockchain itself. Although they are an NFT collectible, these characters have a story behind them unlike any other.

These characters and their world differ from the rest. First of all, their world isn’t exactly “real”. In the year 2222, the earth has been drained of its resources, and human beings are jaded so they have disowned their responsibility as stewards of the earth. As a consequence they spend their days being bored and welcome the new day through extravagant parties. A world-renowned Dj has been hosting parties in exotic locations throughout what is left of the natural world. Not only is this DJ a creator of worlds in the metaverse, but also bridges them via his real life secret concerts.

To bring in the New Year this DJ hosted a party in an undisclosed forest in the matter-world. In this forest there were 12 Capo dei Capi, Boss of Bosses, overseeing 9,999 of their species living comfortably in the forests feeding off the bounty of Mother Earth like grass, hemp, bamboo and beyond. The varying species lived safely in the forest until a group of humans having an afterparty from a nearby nightclub left the fire burning, destroying 96% of their forest, and driving animals nearly to absolute extinction.

The Black Hand had enough of the shenanigans from humans and organized the forest, creating a syndicate of animals along with their families to take back the nightclubs to ensure humans party responsibly and once again teach humans how to steward the Earth responsibly.

To fund their global initiative to hit their NET ZERO goals and reforest the Earth, the Munchie Mafia grew hemp for its versatility and utility to create 25,000+ unique products. They used the funds to plant trees, rebuilt organic agricultural farms, invested in alternative innovative energy sources and unique ways to build eco-friendly cities and beyond. However, first they had to rally humanity. With no hope left for a resource-drained Earth the notorious Muchie Mafia Night Club rose to the occasion and started stewarding the planet responsibly, and bossed humans around until they got their act together.
